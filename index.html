Our classes

Kendo
剣道

Modern Japanese martial art of fencing with two-handed bamboo swords, originally developed as a safe form of sword training for samurai.

Iaido
居合道

is a Japanese martial art that emphasizes being aware and capable of quickly drawing the sword and responding to sudden attacks.

About our school

Our dojo is a space where students immerse themselves in the art of fencing. It is equipped with all the necessary facilities to provide a safe and focused environment for mastering techniques.

Our coaches are experienced professionals with years of competitive and teaching experience. They provide personalized training, focusing on technique, strategy, and discipline, helping students reach their full potential.

We provide our own equipment for kendo training, ensuring safety and quality for all students. Additionally, we have our own shop offering a selection of books and swords, catering to those looking to deepen their knowledge and skills in the art of kendo.
