Authentic Samurai Swords

A Japanese sword with a curved blade is called a katana. The kanji utilized to type in "katana" includes a variety of conceivable meanings. The most widely used character is 刀, sometimes known as "sword," "blade," or "sabre." The two other prevalent writings are 斬刀 and 大刀.

Our collection boasts a wide variety of authentic Samurai swords or Nihonto, offering an intimate glimpse into the Samurai ethos. Each katana, wakizashi, and tanto in our collection is a testament to the skillful art of Japanese swordsmithing. Crafted from traditional Tamahagane steel, T10, or high carbon through a rigorous process of sword forging, these swords embody the spirit of Bushido and the warrior's life.
