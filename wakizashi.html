What is a Wakizashi

The blade length of the wakizashi (脇差), a traditional Japanese short sword, normally ranges from 30 to 60 cm. It was frequently worn alongside the katana to create the "daisho," a pair known as the "side arm" of the samurai. The wakizashi, which was more than simply a weapon but also a representation of a samurai's dignity and social standing, was crucial to the seppuku rite of suicide.
